About Me

I'm a Quality Assurance Engineer passionate about delivering reliable, high‑quality digital products through meticulous testing and continuous improvement. My favorite work lies at the intersection of technology and user experience, ensuring that every feature not only functions as intended but also provides a seamless and trustworthy experience for end users.

Currently, I specialize in designing and executing comprehensive test strategies, from manual exploratory testing to automated frameworks, to help teams ship confidently at scale. I collaborate closely with developers and product managers to identify potential issues early, advocate for best practices, and maintain a culture of quality throughout the development lifecycle.

In the past, I've had the opportunity to work across diverse industries and teams — from startups building MVPs to enterprise‑level platforms — where I focused on improving release processes, reducing defects in production, and implementing automation pipelines that saved countless hours of regression testing. During this time, I also had the chance to lead QA teams in both manual and automation testing, guiding them to deliver consistent, high‑quality results.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring new testing tools, offering free classes to teach QA from basic to advanced levels, spending quality time with my wife and kids, and playing Dota.
